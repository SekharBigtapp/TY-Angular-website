Accrediation with Traditional Yoga


International Yoga Alliance is a charity-based Yoga association with its headquarters in the USA. The work of the Alliance is carried out by those who have dedicated their lives to yoga and its purity, and not as a full-time business occupation or to gain name and fame, but, like Saint Patanjali, whose actual name is not known to anyone, these volunteers remain anonymous. IYA is a union of world Yoga enthusiasts that is intended for true promoters of Yoga, aimed at bringing all yoga communities and individuals across the world under one umbrella ‘Vasudaika Kutumbham’ / world as one family.

IYA is the authentic organisation with strong roots in the Himalayas and associations with traditional ashrams and gompas, and their heads. These are the places where those great yogis from Swamy Vivekananda to Tirumalai Krishnamacharya got their education and where great figures like Annie Besant and Rudolf Steiner experienced the reality beyond their imagination and went on to become creators of traditions, Steiner establishing Waldorf education/Steiner Schools with Emil Molt. The Himalayan traditions are the origins of all the healing teachings and the place where these nnovators gained their knowledge.

IYA is committed to represent traditions with an open mind that does not levy any subscription fee from its members. It allows its registered members to promote and teach yoga as certified, indicating that they have the base and background in different formats and in a wide spectrum of yoga, as preserved for thousands of years, and which has flowed from generation to generation up to the modern contemporary traditions.

The Alliance’s aim is not the promotion of yoga in the name of associations which, directly or indirectly collect money in the name of promotion of yoga. That is far from the tradition which has existed for thousands of years promoting yoga as a selfless activity. IYA is neither a business nor a livelihood entity for anyone promoting yoga, it is for the purpose of serving the yoga community with soul in its heart.

IYA is an organisation which, unlike other associations, issues authentic certifications to schools, teachers, studios and ashrams the world over, to whomsoever is committed and has proven that they are practicing or teaching yoga or promoting yoga in the right spirit. We are proud to be associated with these teachers, schools, studios and ashrams, and we issue certificates upon registration in turn
