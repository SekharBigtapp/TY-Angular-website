Our Aim


Protect         Revive         Disseminate

original, undiluted and complete yogic knowledge, both theory and practice in its purest form for the benifit of the public and public wellbeing of entire world.

The courses are all run as charitable not-for profit activity.

The Charity


The charity has an established connection to India, primarily to guarantee its authenticity at source but also as a means to safeguard the continuation of traditional knowledge in its original cultural setting for the benefit of future generations.

Our charities in the US and UK make charitable grants to Yoga Healing Foundation India, with whom we have established collaborative relationship, to organize and deliver programs that align to our aims and objectives.

Programs Funded


The few programs we fund include
• Large scale free public yoga and health awareness workshops. These take place outdoors in public parks and are attended by public at large per year.
• Free symposiums and seminars addressing particular topics; free guest lectures for particular institutions, organisations and professional communities
• Yoga centers have been hired and established throughout the city to provide an ongoing resource for teachers and students. These provide morning and evening yoga classes for local communities.
• Residential meditation courses.
• Free certified online yogi training programs

• Preserving scriptures, ancient techniques and discourses digitally
• Supplying Neutraceuticals to the needy and well deserved.

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how we utilize personal information that is collected at this site. We advise you to read this policy before submitting any personal information or using the site.

Information Collection:

At our website, we collect personally identifiable information, such as names, postal addresses, and email addresses, only when provided voluntarily by our visitors. The information you provide is used solely to fulfill your specific request, unless you grant us permission to use it in another way, such as adding you to a mailing list.

Cookie/Tracking Technology:

Our site may utilize cookie and tracking technology depending on the features available. Cookie and tracking technology are useful for gathering information such as browser type, operating system, and the number of visitors to the site, as well as for understanding how visitors use the site. Cookies can also be helpful in customizing the site for visitors. It is important to note that while cookies and other tracking technologies cannot collect personal information, if you have provided personally identifiable information previously, cookies may be linked to this information. Aggregate cookie and tracking data may be shared with third parties.

Processing of Usage Data:

Processing of usage data for both web visitors and customers may be undertaken by us. This usage data could consist of various pieces of information, such as your IP address, browser type and version, geographical location, operating system, referral source, page views, length of visit, and website navigation paths. Additionally, data about the frequency, timing, and pattern of your service use could also be collected. The sources of this usage data include Google Analytics, Google Adwords, Facebook Advertising, and Mailchimp. We may process this usage data for the purpose of analyzing the utilization of our website and services. Our legal basis for processing this data is either explicit consent from users or our legitimate interests, specifically the monitoring and improvement of our website and services.

Distribution of Information:

We may share information with governmental agencies or other companies in order to assist us in fraud prevention or investigation. This may occur when it is either permitted or required by law or when we are attempting to protect against or prevent actual or potential fraud or unauthorized transactions, or investigating fraud that has already taken place. Please note that this information is not provided to these companies for marketing purposes.

Commitment to Data Security:

Your personally identifiable information is kept secure. Only authorized volunteers, agents and contractors (who have agreed to keep information secure and confidential) have access to this information.

Full Disclosure in Ashram Culture:

In the context of Ashram culture, seeking a Guru indicates a sincere desire on the part of a Yogi Aspirant to leave behind their cultural and intellectual baggage and be completely transparent with the teacher. This enables the teacher to provide guidance on all aspects of life. It is important to note that if you are selected for any of the Yoga Courses offered by Traditional Yoga, intentionally withholding information that may impede your progress on the path is not permitted. Please be aware that failure to fully disclose pertinent information may result in removal from the program(s). The purpose of requesting this information is to facilitate your growth on the path and help you heal body, mind, and soul.

Updates/ Modifications:

Your use of the site indicates your acceptance of the practices outlined in this privacy policy. Although these practices may be revised, any modifications will be published and will only be applied to activities and information on a prospective, not retrospective, basis. It is recommended that you review the privacy policy each time you visit the site to ensure your understanding of how your personal information will be used. Please note that the privacy practices stated in this policy are specific to this website only. If you visit other websites through links on our site, please refer to the privacy policies published on those sites.
